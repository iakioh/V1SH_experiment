Documentation of any design choices different to Popper, 2003

Changes: 
- I don't show the Gabor patch stimulus exactly for the given time, but only for a certain amount of frames = round(refresh_rate * duration)
- from the paper it is not apparant, where the single rows were shown. I decided to show them at the center, since that's where the participant looks according to previous fixation.
- I have a 200ms break in between fixation and stimulus to avoid any after-images and have a clear transition
